Bonus Changer App is designed to be user-friendly and intuitive. To use the app, simply click the 'Change' button to generate new random values for the weapon statistics.
The Attack Value, Critical Chance, Attack Speed, and Sharpness will be updated accordingly. The color-coded visuals provide instant feedback on the changes, with green indicating an increase, red indicating a decrease, and gray representing a neutral value. The app incorporates a cooldown timer to prevent excessive modifications and maintain gameplay balance.

After clicking the 'Change' button, a timer will start, and the button will be disabled until the cooldown period elapses. This ensures a fair and controlled gameplay experience.
